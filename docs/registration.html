Registration

Instructions for registration

Registration Policy

  • Paper Presentation: All papers must be presented at the conference, preferably in person as Oral or Poster presentations (according to the final Program Chairs' notification).
  • Attendance: In-site attendance is strongly recommended as the conference is meant to be an opportunity for scholars to connect, share insights, and reinforce the sense of community that has always been at the heart of this conference. However, remote attendance is permitted for those unable to attend in person due to significant reasons, ensuring broader community connection and sharing.
  • Registration Requirement: All conference attendees, including paper presenters, poster presenters, and general participants, must register for the conference.
  • Author Registration: At least one author for each accepted paper must register for the NLDB 2025 Conference at the in-person fees rate Automating E&M Coding in Medical Documentation: The Rule-Based Approach (even if the paper is presented remotely) by the registration deadline for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings and the program.
  • On-site registration is not permitted. Authors must register via the online platform.

Fees

Conference registration fees include NLDB conference, Coffee breaks, and Social Dinner. Extra tickets for the social dinner can be purchased at the time of the registration.
