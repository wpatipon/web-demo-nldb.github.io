Instructions for registration

Registration Policy

  • Paper Presentation: All papers must be presented at the conference, in person as Oral Presentation.
  • Attendance: Onsite attendance is strongly recommended as the conference is meant to be an opportunity for scholars to connect, share insights, and reinforce the sense of community that has always been at the heart of this conference.
  • Author Registration: At least one author for each accepted paper must register for the NLDB 2025 Conference May 9, 2025, 23:59 (AoE) with the Regular Fee.
  • On-site registration is not permitted. Authors must register via the online platform.

Fees

* At least one author of each accepted paper must register with the Regular Fee.

Early Registration by May 14, 2025, 23:59 (JST):
  • Regular: 65,000 JPY
  • Student: 50,000 JPY
Late Registration:
  • Regular: 80,000 JPY
  • Student: 65,000 JPY
Extra Ticket:
  • Banquet only: 15,000 JPY
Conference registration fees include NLDB conference, Coffee breaks, and Banquet. Extra tickets for the banquet can be purchased at the time of the registration.

Visa Support & Student Proof


Visa Support

To those who may need visa support, applicants must meet all of the following conditions to request our visa documentation:

  • You have completed the online registration.
  • Your registration fee have been paid in full.
  • Please send an email to the Visa Support Desk at nldb2025@easychair.org. The details are as follows:
    For example, [NLDB 2025] Visa Support Request - xxxxxx_John Doe

Student Proof

  • If you are a student please send proof to nldb2025@easychair.org. The details are as follows:

    For example, [NLDB 2025] Student Proof - John Doe
    Please attach a scanned copy of your Student ID card with the filename, John_Doe.pdf
