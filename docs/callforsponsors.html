About NLDB

The 30th Annual International Conference on Natural Language & Information Systems (NLDB 2025) will take place in Kanazawa, Japan, from July 4-6, 2025. Since 1995, NLDB has been a key forum for bridging Natural Language Processing (NLP) with Databases and Information Systems. This year's conference will spotlight cutting-edge advances—from Large Language Models and conversational AI to multimodal systems and bias-aware AI—all driving real-world applications like chatbots, recommender systems, and sentiment analysis. NLDB 2025 invites researchers, industry experts, and practitioners to share innovative work and explore how NLP is transforming the future of information systems.

Why Sponsor NLDB 2025?

By sponsoring NLDB 2025, your organization gains visibility among top professionals and shows commitment to technological advancement in natural language generation. The conference will be an in-person event, ensuring maximum interaction and exposure.
